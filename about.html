About us

Over 15 Years of Experience

Srisupap Parawood Company Limited has provided the rubber wood sawn timber for more than 15 years. The company is located at Ban Khlong Teng, Muang District, Trang. With the strong determination of the local owner, the quality of rubber wood sawn timber has been developed to be acceptable at the forefront of both domestic and foreign customers.

Throughout the years, the company has been continuously accumulating experiences, developing the personnel and production technology. Starting from the selection of timber materials from the plantation, through sawing, wood impregnation and kiln drying by quality inspection of skilled technicians at every step. As a result, our products are recognized as good quality, great size, beautiful color, and satisfied customers' wood texture.

Currently, the company is continuously developing rubber products, such as compressed rubber wood, furniture parts, stair components, wood chip, wood pellet, briquette charcoal.
